Silly Jokes

There are many funny jokes that we have heard. Some of the best are the silly ones, often called Dad Jokes! Here are a few examples.

  • Q. What do you call an angry carrot?
    A. A steamed veggie.

  • Q. What has more lives than a cat?
    A. A frog, because it croaks every day.

  • Q. What does a baby computer call his father?
    A. Data!
